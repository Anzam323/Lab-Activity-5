School of Management

The School of management is a university-level institution that confers degrees in management. The School of management is a comprehensive concept for leading and operating an organization, aimed at continually improving performance by focusing on customers while addressing the needs of all stakeholders. Currently, SOM offers PHD by research, Master in Management, and BS Aggribusiness Economics.
