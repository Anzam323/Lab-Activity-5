College of Humanities and Social Sciences

The College of Humanities and Social Sciences is one of the two colleges that evolved out of the first undergraduate unit of UP in Mindanao, the College of Arts and Sciences. Its initial undergraduate offerings were BA English (Creative Writing) and BA Social Science (Behavioral Science). Currently, CHSS has four undergraduate offerings, namely BS Anthropology, BA Communication Arts, BA English (Creative Writing), and BS Architecture.
